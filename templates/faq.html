Frequently Asked Questions

What is Kryptos?

Kryptos is an interactive quiz platform that allows users to test their knowledge on various topics in a fun and engaging way.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button and filling in the required information. It's quick and easy!

What topics are available for quizzes?

We offer quizzes on a variety of topics including cryptography, web development, logic-based mathematics, and more! Check back often for new categories.

How can I reset my password?

If you forget your password, you can reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Follow the instructions sent to your email.

Who can I contact for support?

You can reach out to our support team via the contact form on our website. We're here to help you with any questions or issues!

Can I take quizzes on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive, allowing you to take quizzes on your smartphone or tablet anytime, anywhere.

How are quiz scores calculated?

Your score is calculated based on the number of correct answers you provide. Each correct answer earns you points, and your total score is displayed at the end of the quiz.

Are there any time limits for quizzes?

Yes, each quiz has a time limit to keep things exciting! You'll see a timer counting down as you answer questions.

Can I review my quiz results later?

Yes! After completing a quiz, you can review your results and see which questions you got right or wrong.

Is there a way to challenge my friends?

Currently, we don't have a direct challenge feature, but you can share your scores and quiz results with friends to see who knows more!
